I am a final-year PhD student in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University advised by Nina Balcan and Tuomas Sandholm. My research is at the interface of mechanism and market design, optimization and operations research (speficially integer programming), and machine learning.

In Summer 2022 I worked on recommender systems at Google Research as a student researcher with Craig Boutilier and Martin Mladenov. I received a B.S. in math and computer science from Caltech in 2019.
